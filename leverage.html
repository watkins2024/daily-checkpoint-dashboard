Quick reference

  • Micro‑steps: Tick Plan → Do (10‑min) → Proof. Repeat weekly until the rung is truly done.
  • Mark a milestone: Use the button beside the current rung. It auto‑logs an action and resets micro‑steps for that rung.
  • Evidence: One factual sentence (e.g., “Sold first $40 service”).
  • Energy: Rate the weekly energy/pressure for this pathway (0–100). Lower = heavy; use as an early‑warning signal.
  • Exit threshold: The big bar glows as you approach 60% (independence baseline).
